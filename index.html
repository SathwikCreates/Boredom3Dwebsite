📜 Manifesto

I started coding because I was curious. I kept coding because it turned out to be a way to say things, solve problems, and sometimes even make people laugh (yeah, I made a dad joke extension — no regrets).

Right now, I’m a Computer Science student at Pragati Engineering College. But outside of classes, I’m usually experimenting — making tools for students like the EPASS app, building things like attendance trackers, and messing around with creative projects that help people ask someone out (because hey, why not make that easier too?).

This site is a space where I share what I’m building, what I’m learning, and where I’ve messed up along the way. It's less of a "portfolio" and more of a work-in-progress journal — and I’m cool with that.
